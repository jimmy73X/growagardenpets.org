GARDEN PET GUIDE

How to Get Pets in Grow a Garden

Discover the best ways to collect and nurture your garden companions

Collect Adorable Pets

Players can collect up to 86 adorable pets in the game, though only 60 of them can be stored at a time (unless you've bought extra pet storage), and just a few can be actively used on the farm.

Raphael's Stall

The main way to get pets in Grow a Garden is by opening Pet Eggs, which are available through Raphael's Stall or special event shops.

Tip: Developers often give out free new pets during live events!
