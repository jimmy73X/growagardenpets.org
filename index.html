Grow a Garden: All Pets and What They Do

Pets are an essential part of your gardening journey in Grow a Garden. Each pet has unique abilities that can help you nurture your plants, protect your garden, and earn more rewards. Here's a complete guide to all available pets and their functions.

Why Pets Matter

Pets in Grow a Garden serve multiple purposes. They can speed up plant growth, protect against pests, increase harvest yields, and even help you discover rare plants. Choosing the right combination of pets can significantly boost your gardening efficiency.

Pet Care and Happiness

Keeping your pets happy is crucial for maximizing their benefits. Happy pets provide 30% more effective abilities compared to neglected pets. Here's how to maintain pet happiness:

Daily Interaction

Pet, feed, and play with your pets daily to keep their happiness high. Each interaction adds +5% to happiness.

Comfortable Habitat

Upgrade living spaces with decorations and comfort items. Better habitats increase happiness cap by 20%.

Special Treats

Use rare treats for temporary boosts. Magic cookies can instantly max happiness for 24 hours.
